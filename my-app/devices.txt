Alliteration: repetition of the same consonant sound
Assonance: repetition of the vowel sound
Sibilance: repetition of the 's' sound
Pastoral: idealising country life; Sonnet 18: "Shall I compare thee to a summer's day" (Shakespeare, line 1)
Syntactic Parallelism: repetition of (part of) lines that are not phonologically identified, but have the same syntactical structure; She Walks in Beauty: "One shade the more, one ray the less," (Lord Byron, line 7)
